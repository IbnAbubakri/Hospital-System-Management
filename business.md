# MediCore Hospital Management System - Business Strategy

## Executive Summary

MediCore is a comprehensive, cloud-ready hospital management system built with modern technology (Next.js 16, TypeScript, Ant Design). This document outlines monetization strategies, target markets, pricing models, and growth roadmap for turning this into a profitable SaaS business.

---

## 1. Business Model Options

### Option A: SaaS Subscription (Recommended)

**How it works:** Host the application on cloud infrastructure (Vercel/AWS) and charge monthly/annual subscriptions.

**Revenue Streams:**
- Monthly subscriptions per facility
- Per-user licensing
- Add-on modules
- Implementation & training fees

**Advantages:**
- Recurring revenue (MRR/ARR)
- Scalable with minimal additional cost
- Centralized updates & security
- Lower barrier to entry for customers

**Estimated Pricing (Nigeria Market):**
| Tier | Price/Month | Features |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Starter | ₦50,000 | Up to 10 staff, basic modules |
| Professional | ₦150,000 | Up to 50 staff, all modules |
| Enterprise | ₦500,000+ | Unlimited staff, custom features, dedicated support |

---

### Option B: White-Label Licensing

**How it works:** Sell the software with the client's branding to hospitals/clinics who want to own the system.

**Revenue Streams:**
- One-time license fee: ₦5,000,000 - ₦20,000,000
- Annual maintenance: 15-20% of license fee
- Customization fees

**Advantages:**
- Higher upfront revenue
- Client owns the system
- Good for hospitals with IT teams

---

### Option C: Hybrid Model

**How it works:** Offer both SaaS subscription and self-hosted options.

**Benefits:**
- Appeal to different customer segments
- Lower risk for clients (try before they buy)
- Multiple revenue streams

## 2. Target Markets

### Primary Market: Nigeria

**Why Nigeria?**
- Population: 200M+ people
- Growing middle class with health insurance
- Government push for digital healthcare
- Limited competition in affordable HMS solutions
- Massive shortage of hospital management software

**Target Segments:**
1. **Private Hospitals** (₦50,000 - ₦10M budget)
   - 500+ private hospitals in Lagos alone
   - Average: 20-50 bed facilities
   - Need: Appointment, billing, patient records

2. **Clinics** (₦20,000 - ₦100,000 budget)
   - 5,000+ clinics in Nigeria
   - Single/multi-doctor practices
   - Need: Simple patient management, billing

3. **Specialty Centers** (₦200,000+ budget)
   - Eye clinics, dental, fertility, etc.
   - Need: Specialized modules

### Secondary Markets

1. **West Africa** (Ghana, Kenya, South Africa)
   - Similar healthcare challenges
   - English-speaking populations
   - Expansion potential

2. **East Africa** 
   - Growing healthcare sector
   - Mobile-first users (Opportunity for PWA)
